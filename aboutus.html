Project Overview

KAPITAL is a web development project focused on financial planning and wealth literacy. The platform is designed to help users manage their personal finances by tracking their expenses and assets while providing educational resources on wealth management.

Motivation & Inspiration

Our team is passionate about financial literacy because we believe it's a vital skill that everyone should have. We were inspired by the lack of accessible financial education resources and wanted to create something that could make a difference in people's lives by helping them take control of their finances.
